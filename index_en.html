NAME

sysarmy - El soporte de los que dan soporte.

SYNOPSIS

sysarmy [options]

DESCRIPTION

sysarmy es la comunidad Argentina de sistemas, que nuclea a todos los profesionales del área para favorecer el contacto y el intercambio de conocimiento de manera informal.

OPTIONS

  • --irc

    Our IRC channel is where we gather every day, at any hour. No matter if it is for technical questions or just for fun: #sysarmy at freenode.net

  • --help

    Technical Q&A site where users also get points for each interaction.

  • --blog

    In our blog we publish news and articles, related to our community and IT in general.

  • --facebook

    You can like our Facebook page and get news, how-to's, articles and funny posts.

  • --twitter

    You can also follow us on @sysarmy.

  • --linkedin

    In this group you will find job postings.

  • --googleplus

    We organize our events and meetups using meetup.com.

  • --mailinglist

    En la mailing list you can find technical questions and announcements.

  • --adminbirras

    We get together once a month at Genk Beer House. Check with us in IRC or any of the previous links for the schedule.

BUGS

It's not a good idea to attend AdminBeers while being on-call. Don't drink and type.
