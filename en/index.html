NAME

sysarmy - Support for those who give support.

SYNOPSIS

sysarmy --lang=[es|en] [options]

DESCRIPTION

Sysarmy is the Systems Community that brings together all IT professionals for knowledge exchange and fun (not necessarily in that order)

OPTIONS

  • --irc

    Our IRC channel is where we gather every day, at any hour. No matter if it is for technical questions or just for fun: #sysarmy at libera.chat

  • --discord

    It's the 21st century and some people prefer to use Discord over IRC (we can't still understand why). Anyway, you can join us!

  • --slack

    Same as above, but some people prefer to use Slack over Discord and IRC. Anyway, you can join us!

  • --polemicaenvar

    We host a podcast/videocast show where each month we cover news and a special subject. Follow us on YouTube and Spotify.

  • --help

    Technical Q&A site where users also get points for each interaction.

  • --blog

    In our blog we publish news and articles, related to our community and IT in general.

  • --twitter

    You can also follow us on @sysarmy.

  • --instagram

    Wanna see what kind of food IT people eat? we have an Instagram account as well!

  • --linkedin

    In this group you will find job postings.

  • --store

    Want it. Need it. You got it at @sysarmy's online shop.

BUGS

It's not a good idea to attend AdminBeers while being on-call. Don't drink and type.
